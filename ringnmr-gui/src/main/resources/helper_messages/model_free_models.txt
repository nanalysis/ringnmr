Model 2sf:
Extended model-free formalism of Clore et. al., featuring 2 distinct local motions: fast (τf < 150ps) and slow (τs >= 150ps).

Model 2s:
Truncated form of model 2sf, with τf = 0s.

Model 1f:
Lipari-Szabo model-free formalism, with a single fast local motion (τf < 150ps).

Model 1s:
Lipari-Szabo model-free formalism, with a single slow local motion (τs >= 150ps).

Model 1:
Truncated form of model 1f, with τf = 0s.

Model 1sf:
Applicable to deuterated methyl fitting only.
Model 2sf with S²f set to 1 (it is scaled by 1/9 in the equation).
